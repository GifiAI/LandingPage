GifiAI

GifiAI is our first innovative AI project that specializes in text-to-GIF generation.
Built upon the foundations of fine-tuning the powerful Make-A-Video model, GifiAI harnesses the capabilities of state-of-the-art deep learning and natural language processing techniques to transform text into visually stunning and captivating GIF animations.
With a focus on customization, realism, and exceptional visual fidelity, GifiAI empowers individuals, marketers, content creators, and educators to elevate their communication, storytelling, and engagement through dynamic and expressive GIF animations.

During the initial training stage, we extended a pretrained latent-diffusion inpainting image generation model by introducing a pseudo 3D convolution approach. This technique enables separate convolutions across the spatial dimension in 2D and the temporal dimension in 1D, allowing the model to capture spatial and temporal information effectively.

Additionally, we incorporated temporal attention through self-attention mechanisms that independently attend to the temporal dimension, enhancing the model's ability to capture temporal dependencies within the generated images. The combination of pseudo 3D convolution and temporal attention improves the model's performance in generating high-quality, coherent images while preserving temporal consistency in video sequences. Moving forward, these advancements set the stage for further refinements and enhancements in the subsequent stages of training for our image generation project.
